## What Data Is Collected

I do not directly collect, store or process any personal data on this website.

However, this site uses **Google Analytics 4 (GA4)**, a web analytics service provided by Google LLC. GA4 collects the following data automatically when you visit this site:

- Approximate geographic location (country/city level)
- Browser type and version
- Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
- Operating system
- Pages visited and time spent
- Referring website (how you arrived at this site)

Google Analytics 4 is configured to **not** collect full IP addresses. IP addresses are truncated by Google before storage.
